# Lightweaver — Handoff Card (printable)

Single double-sided business-card or postcard. Tape inside the box, slip into
an envelope, or just hand it to the customer. Keep it short. Plain language.

---

## Front

```
        L I G H T W E A V E R

  Your piece, your light.


  Plug it in.
  The light comes on.

  Turn the knob —  brighter or dimmer
  Press the knob —  next pattern
```

## Back

```
  WANT MORE CONTROL?

  Open WiFi on your phone.
  Look for  Lightweaver-XXXX
  Tap it. Follow the setup screen.

  After that, anytime you're on
  your home WiFi:

         lightweaver.local

  Open it in any browser.
  Patterns, colors, brightness.


  Questions? hello@mandalacodes.com
  More patterns: led.mandalacodes.com
```

---

## Design notes

- Warm white paper, deep ink. Not glossy.
- Typography should match the piece's identity — same wordmark, same letter spacing.
- The two URLs are the only things that need to be exactly right; everything else can be loosened.
- If the customer is buying multiple pieces, write each piece's hostname in pen on the back (the default is `lightweaver` but you can rename per piece in the designer before handoff).

## Print sizing

- Postcard 4×6": this fits comfortably with room to breathe.
- Business card 3.5×2": works as front-only with the URL on the back. Tighter but still readable.

## Versions to print

For an in-person sale, I'd print 25 at a time. Cheap from any local print shop or VistaPrint.
